Overview

In February 2025, I was a participant with my team in the MinneHack 24‑hour hackathon. We built a habit tracking system that allows two users to track their habits, streaks, and “mend events” through a microcontroller‑hosted website. Users also had a button that was connected to a breadboard that would log a "habit event" when presesed, and would light up a corresponding LED. This process could easily be extended to a mobile app.

My Role

With such a short timeline, I focused on the embedded side of the project. I did all work related to the ESP32 microcontroller, including setting up the web server, handling button presses, and logging events.
